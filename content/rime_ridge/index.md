The Rime Ridge is a dense mountain range thick with fog that tends to freeze into hard rime ice upon all surfaces.
This is inspired by the Misty Mountains of the Hobbit.

## Terrain and Biome

Dense mountain range thick with fog.
Everything is rime covered.

Takes **x3 typical travel time** to travel through the Rime Ridge hex.
Can take longer depending on the weather and whether you follow the typical trails and mountain passes.

## Weather

1/10 chance the fog all froze over night leaving a rare clear morning only to be soon filled with fog come noon.

## Rumours

These mountains are perilous.

## Random Encounters

### Roaming Characters in the Rime Ridge

- Nest of **Aeon**
- Behemoth Bears
    - Big Bear lives with his wife, a normal sized Grizzly and their cub, notably they stay within a cottage.
- **The Wind Elementals**
    - Vagrant Wind
    - Raging Wing
    - Whispering Wind
- **Gorcs**: The hob goblin orcs of Rankin/Bass's animated films "Hobbit" and "The Return of the King".
    - Think brachycephalic cats and bull dog face meets stocky boar with sharp teeth and tusks.
        - oinks, barks, and purrs
    - They are similar to the thick and stocky moblins of "The Legend of Zelda".
    - **The Great Gorc** rules **Gorcington** hidden within the Rime Ridge
    - The Gorc-Bane Weapons: All burn in radiant fire in the presence of gorcs, and glow in the presence of orcs, goblins, and cats.
        - Gorcrend: A Zweihander. with parierhaken
        - Gorcrist: A hand and a half sword
        - Beater: A mace
- **Ramkins**: Humanoid bodies with the head of a ram with curling horns, as well as the hind legs and hooves of a ram. Their forearms are much more wooly and end with larger hands than a human and have three fingers that end similarly to deer toes.
    - A ramekin is always found among their belongings, even when travelling.
- **Tuskers**: Huge tusked trolls dwell in these mountains.
    - They thrive within the mists and caves of Rime Ridge where they avoid the sun which would turn them to stone
- **Catragon**: Rime Ridge has a feral catragon problem.
- **Mountain Elementals**: They'll pelt rocks at each other and passerbys for a fun game of catch.

## Locations within the Rime Ridge

- The Rime Ridge : an actual mountain ridge that connects various mountains in the middle.
    - Walking atop the snow covered ridge lets you see above the rime and fog.
- Blind Man's Bluff
- Gorcington
- Tusker Holes
- A Dwarf fortress hidden in the mountains.
